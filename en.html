ABOUT COMPANY

Global Sourcing,
Reliable Manufacturing

I.S.T Co., Ltd. is a specialized company providing electric components, harness and cable manufacturing and distribution, as well as global sourcing services.

Since its establishment in 2000, I.S.T has supplied stable products and services to various domestic and overseas industries, placing quality management and customer satisfaction as its top priorities.
